"We are not human beings have a spiritual experience. We are spiritual beings having a human experience."

~Pierre Teilhard de Chardin

When I first heard this quoate it immediately resonated with me and I knew I was exactly where I was meant to be at that moment in life, to hear that quote, and reaffirm some of my most recent, and wonderous, visions, ideations and beliefs I had been experiencing!

I am spiritual, I believe in a higher power, whom I choose to address as God, but it is my own interpretation, my own personal ideas, and my own personal belief system that I choode to have faith in. I do not need to explain every little detail of my beliefs, because they may be extremely differant than someone elses, and that is perfectly okay too! I think it is extremely important to have faith in something bigger than yourself, but once again it is a personl ideology and perception of whatever or whomeever each individual chooses to believe in, and all that matters is that it provides hope, faith and happiness!

My journey has been long and steady and filled with lot's of trials and tibulations, and sometimes I never knew what it was I was suppose to learn from those experiences, until later in life. When I can finally say,"Awww, now that makes sense!" Those are great moments! My personal experience is that everything happens for a reason, whether we realise it at that exact moment in time, or later in life during those Aha moments!

I founded New Found Oasis in hopes of encouraging like minded indidvidals to share experience, insights and support to one another while exploring their own personal paths to their own sacred oasis within!
